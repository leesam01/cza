Project Pack No. 06 Reminder
The Dog Days of Summer" – a perfect time for a Project Pack!

We are just about to hit the last stretch of the dogs days of summer here in the Northern Hemisphere. It's hot. It's humid and folks are in slow motion. At this time of year, we all seem to slow down, indulge in some time off or away, grab a good beach novel or even better our Zentangle supplies.

What a perfect time to indulge in some serious tangle time with a new Project Pack.

The supplies for Project Pack No.06 were released about a month ago and we are gearing up to release the videos beginning next Thursday August 8th.

Each Project Pack series is curated behind a theme or focus. 

Project Pack 06 is all about the NO MISTAKES philosophy. 
The "No Mistakes" philosophy has been an integral part of the Zentangle Method since its beginning. "No Mistakes" is a mantra we use in our Zentangle practice that allows us to see every line as an opportunity and embrace every mark that is ours as artists. Whether intentional or not, with this perspective, one can learn to see beauty and potential in whatever mark we make and whatever path our artistic journey takes. This project pack includes a  special surprise  that is inspired by one specific story in Zentangle history that exemplifies the Zentangle "No Mistakes" philosophy.

This series pulls at the heart strings of the method and allows us to tangle through some stories that really illustrate how the Zentangle method can change ones perspective and empower the artist within us all.

We are so excited to see what you all do with these materials and series of tutorials.

We hope you enjoy exploring these tutorials yourself and If you are actively teaching, we encourage you to use this Project Pack as a platform for working with your students.

We like to tell CZTs how we plan deliver the content so you can plan ahead if you are using the videos with students or friends. Although it is fun to play along day by day, once the content is announced, it will remain available so you can watch and use the videos at your own pace. 

Each of the videos range between 10 and 30 minutes.

  • August 11 - Lesson 4

Project Pack No. 06!

沒有錯誤

沒有錯誤 (No Mistakes)」的哲理概念在Zentanlge創建之初就已經成為了禪繞畫方法中的一部分。「沒有錯誤 (No Mistakes)」是我們在練習禪繞畫過程中一句重要的格言，這讓我們能將眼前的每一個線條都視為機會，並像藝術家般擁抱每一個屬於自己的筆劃和標記。

無論是否預先計畫，只要透過這個觀點，大家都可以學習從任一個創作軌跡，或是任何一種藝術之旅中，看到蘊藏在其中的美麗和潛力。材料中的迷你手札設計靈感就是使用禪繞Zentangle歷史中一個別具意義的故事，來詮釋「沒有對錯 (No Mistakes)」這個理念。
