CZT數位資源 CZT Support and Digital Resources

Newsletters

Zentangle HQ sends out two types of newsletters – one to the General Public and one to CZTs only. After seminar, you will be added to both email lists. The CZT newsletter is our main form of communication with CZTs. Please add RickandMaria@zentangle.com to your address book to prevent the newsletters from going to your Spam folder.

Zentangle Blog

Visit Zentangle.com/blogs/blog to stay up to date on all of our Zentangle thoughts, ideas, stories and more.

Zentangle Mosaic

Join us on the Zentangle Mosaic app by downloading it from the Apple App Store/Google Play store. The Mosaic app is a community of tanglers from around the world sharing their Zentangle art and stories.

For assistance with Zentangle Mosaic, please email mosaic@zentangle.com

Zentangle.com

CZTs are given special permission on zentangle.com which allow them to purchase Zentangle products at their CZT discounted price and access the CZT-only CZT Resources section of the website. At the beginning of seminar, your account will be given CZT permissions.

Once you are logged in to your CZT account, you will notice a menu option appear next to Learn, Shop and Share called “CZT Resources”

If at any point you have questions regarding your CZT account, please email julie@zentangle.com.

Helpful Hints:

  • When placing your order, you must log into your CZT Account BEFORE adding items to your cart in order to receive your CZT discount
  • Certain items have a “Size” drop down menu which you should use to select bulk options at a discounted price (i.e. Boxes of 12 pens, Pack of 12 pencils, Bundle of 6 options)
  • To compare Retail vs CZT prices, please view your CZT catalog (also available for download in the CZT Resources section of the website).

Zentangle.events

CZTs can create a public profile in the CZT Directory on zentangle.events. 

  1. Login to your zentangle.events account (this is the account you created when you registered for seminar)
  2. Click Dashboard.
  3. Select Profile.
  4. Update your Profile information. Please note that any information you enter on this page is voluntary and not required. Please only enter the information that you want publicly available.
  5. Click Update at the bottom of the page.

Helpful Hints:

  • For your contact information to appear, please select “Display in List with Contact Info” at the top of the form.
  • If you do not see your city/town in the drop-down menus, please select “other” and a text box will appear for you to add it.
  • If you are not available for classes, it is helpful to write so in your bio.

If you would like to be listed in two different locations, you will need a second account. Please email julie@zentangle.com for assistance.

NOTE: Zentangle.com and Zentangle.events are two different and separate websites. Account details are not synced. Updating information on zentangle.com will not update it on zentangle.events.

CZT Forum

There is a CZT-only forum available on Zentangle for you to connect with other CZTs around the world. This is a great place to ask questions, get advice and share inspiration with your fellow CZTs! Once you are logged in as a CZT, you can navigate to the CZT Forum from the “CZT Resources” Menu.

Zentangle中文禪繞藝術機構 Chinese Zentangle Association, CZA

為能服務廣泛使用中文的大眾，Zentangle, Inc. 已正式授予好心藝建立非營利的華語組織CZA (Chinese Zentangle Association)。

通過建立一個交流禪繞、體驗禪繞、推廣禪繞的平台，為更多使用中文交流的禪繞認證教師CZT、禪繞藝術愛好者和企業機構，提供中文資訊類的專業服務與支持，讓彼此依據各自需求分享與學習，並結合CZT們的力量組織各種形態的活動，以促進CZT們及廣大禪繞畫愛好者之間的交流。
